Introduction and Goals

The idea for this project was to actually render some kind of landscape and after making some research on the theme The Last One I liked the idea of an old locomotive on a wooden bridge. To bind my idea to the actual theme I chose to break the bridge at one end so that when we see the train coming we don't know what is going to happen. Many scenarios are possible. It could be his last trip.. but he could also stop at the last moment!
